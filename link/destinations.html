 Famous Waray Destinations

Biri Islands

The Biri Islands of Northern Samar is the most underrated tourist spot in the Philippines. In the quiet island of Samar is the home to islands and rock formations that seems out of this world. Biri Islands possess unique volcanic rocks, and is one of the cleanest waters on the island.

Tarabangan Falls is a 60-meter waterfall that sits in the preserved forests of Calbayog City. Altho it might take minutes to trek, the view is absolutely breathtaking. Tarabangan Falls has to be the best hidden gem in the Samar region.

Tarabangan Falls

Sohotan River

Sotohon River is absolutely mindblowing for its stoe bridge and limestone formations that is only a kayak ride away. The serene forestry, relaxing atmosphere and calm breeze around the caves will surely soothe and rest your senses, a natural marvel worth the trip.
